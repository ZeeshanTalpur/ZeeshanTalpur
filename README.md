### 🔍 Featured Project — Project NexusPulse

**An end-to-end SaaS business analytics case study, built entirely in Pandas.**

> AetherStack Technologies *(a fictional SaaS company)* is growing fast — more customers, more subscriptions, record revenue — yet profitability keeps declining. My job: dig through raw, messy, unconnected operational exports (billing, payments, refunds, subscriptions, usage, support tickets) and find out **why growth and profit have split apart.**

**What the project covers:**

- 🧹 **Enterprise Data Quality Assessment** — missing values, duplicates, invalid records, business-rule violations, scored per dataset
- 🔗 **Integrated Analytical Data Model** — merging 10 raw sources (customers, subscriptions, invoices, payments, refunds, usage logs, feature usage, support tickets) into one unified structure
- 📊 **Advanced Pandas Analytics** — GroupBy, multi-level aggregation, merge/join, pivot tables, crosstabs, MultiIndex, window functions
- 💡 **Executive KPI Dashboard** — MRR, ARR, CLV, churn rate, refund rate, payment success rate, resolution time, feature adoption
- 🕵️ **Root Cause Analysis** — separating genuine business failures from simple data-quality noise, each finding backed by evidence
- 📝 **Executive Recommendation Report** — key findings, risks, and actions translated for leadership decision-making

**Core question driving the whole case study:**
*Why is the company becoming less profitable even though nearly every growth metric is improving?*
